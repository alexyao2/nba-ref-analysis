How Accurate are NBA Referees?

WhistleRate is an informational project about how referee accuracy and impact can be studied using media records, game context, and point differentials.

Overview

About the website:

Motivation

With the level of dissatisfaction surrounding NBA referee decisions, there's a growing interest in understanding and quantifying referee accuracy.

Data Collection

The data used for this analysis includes foul differentials, experience, and called fouls per game aggregated from nbastuffer.com.

Primary read

The graph does not prove referee bias, but it highlights referee-season profiles whose foul differential and adjusted home-bias index are unusual enough to justify deeper game-level review.

Center vs. edge

Profiles closer to the center look more baseline. Profiles farther from the center are more noteworthy, especially with larger sample sizes.

Mixed quadrants

Dots in the upper-left or lower-right show disagreement between whistle profile and broader home-bias index, which means foul differential alone is not enough to explain the profile.
